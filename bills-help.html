Installation

1. To install a new version of Bill's Office, first go to the "Bill's Office Releases" Google Document.

2. Choose the version to download and click its link. (The current version is recommended).

3. The link will direct you to a folder. Click on the name and click "download" and let it download.

4. When the folder completes its download, double click on it to open it in your files app.

5. In the folder, find the file "bills-office.html". Double-click on it.

6. The file will open in your browser. Bookmark it so you don't have to find it in the folder again.

7. Enjoy! Bill's Office is now at your fingertips!

Changing the background

1. If you want to set the background color to anything you want instead of just dark or light mode, first click the button with the black color.

2. Choose a color in the color picker and press the enter key.
